Choose the shift value. This is the number of characters the encoder will shift each letter.

The Caesar cipher is a simplest and widely known encryption techniques. It is a substitution cipher in which each letter of the secret message is replaced by a letter a certain number of iterations further on in the alphabet.

The shift value is the secret key which is used to 'lock' the message. Both you and the recipient would know this value, and hopefully your enemy would not!
